My Life

Born and raised in Queens, NY. Worked in the kitchen with the aspirations of becoming a great chef and traveling the world to cook and experience culture from all over the globe. Changed paths when reality struck and life required more money and a higher paying profession but keeping cooking to a life long passion at home. From culinary to tradesman in a couple of months I was quickly on my way onto an HVAC career and moving to California with the person I thought I would spend my life with. While in California I decided to follow a career path that has always fascinated me which is th world of coding and web development. Thats where i found the UCSD Extension Bootcamp and decided to change my life as it was falling apart. Currently back in NY as I try to figure it all out and become a professional web developer and figure out my life.

Hobbies

Hobbies include cooking every day as it makes my anxiety rest and my mind to relax and focus on just whats in front of me which is the marriage of different ingredients harmonizing into a gorgeous dish that you eat with your eyes and nose before it even gets touched by a fork or a spoon. Newest hobby to be added to my list that needs to be further practiced to achieve my goals is web development and over all coding.
